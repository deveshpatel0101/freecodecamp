Steve Jobs

Steve Jobs: One of the biggest tech innovator of all time.

Full Name: Steven Paul Jobs

Birth Place: San Francisco, California, U.S.

Born on: February 24, 1955

Died: October 5, 2011

  • Founder, Chairman and CEO of Apple Inc., Investor and Chairman of Pixar and Founder, CEO and Chairman of NeXT.
  • Adopted by Paul and Clara Jobs.
  • Used to play pranks on others
  • Jobs skipped the fifth grade and transferred to the sixth grade at Crittenden Middle School in Mountain View.
  • Jobs enrolled at Reed College in Portland, Oregon. Reed was an expensive school that Paul and Clara could ill afford.
  • Jobs stated that, he slept on the floor in friends' dorm rooms, returned Coke bottles for food money, and got weekly free meals at the local Hare Krishna temple.
  • Drop out of Reed because he did not want to spend his parents' money on it
